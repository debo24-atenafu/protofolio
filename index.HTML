About me

My name is Deborah Atenafu, and I am a 17-year-old Grade 11 student with a strong passion for creativity and technology. I am currently learning web design and development, where I have developed skills in HTML and CSS, and I am continuing to improve my knowledge of JavaScript. I enjoy building simple, structured, and visually appealing websites, and I am motivated to grow into a skilled web developer in the future. In addition, I have started learning French at a basic level, which has helped me develop an interest in languages and communication. I believe that learning different skills, both technical and creative, makes me more versatile and prepared for future opportunities. I am a dedicated and curious learner who is always willing to explore new ideas and improve my abilities. My goal is to continue developing my skills in web development while combining them with my creativity to create meaningful and attractive digital experiences.
